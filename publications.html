Forthcoming Publications & Research

Ongoing Research

Ved Vidya Shodh Sansthan, under Swami Ji's guidance, is continuously engaged in advanced research into various facets of Vedic sciences, Ayurveda, Jyotish, and ancient Indian mathematical traditions.

These research projects aim to unearth lost knowledge and present ancient wisdom in a format accessible to contemporary scholars and enthusiasts alike.

Academic Journals & Articles

Swami Ji regularly contributes scholarly articles to esteemed national and international journals, presenting his unique perspectives on spiritual philosophy, cultural preservation, and interfaith dialogue.

Stay tuned for updates on new research papers and forthcoming editions from the Sansthan's publishing arm.
